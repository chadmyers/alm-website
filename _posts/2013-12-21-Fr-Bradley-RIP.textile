Father Robert Ignatius Bradley, S.J., died today, December 20, 2013, in Los Gatos, California, at the age of 89.  The son of Joseph and Muriel (Wicks) Bradley, he was born on May 15, 1924, in Spokane, Washington.  He was ordained to the priesthood on August 15, 1955, in Louvain, Belgium.  He pursued graduate studies in History at Columbia University in New York, then he received the S.T.D. from the Pontifical University of St. Thomas (Angelicum) in Rome in 1987.  Living in the Diocese of Austin from 1996 to 2013, he often celebrated the Latin Mass at St. Mary Cathedral.  He moved to the Sacred Heart Jesuit Center in Los Gatos, California, on November 19, 2013.  His funeral will take place after Christmas in Spokane, Washington.  His remains will be buried at the Jesuit Cemetery in Spokane. May his soul and the souls of all the faithful departed, through the mercy of God, rest in peace.
 
Please keep Betty and Father's two remaining brothers and very large extended family in prayer.
